The Mole

Players: 0

Game Rules

Take turns describing the word you see with a single word clue.

The Mole must try to blend in with the other players.

After each round, players can discuss and vote on who they think the Mole is.

At least half the players must agree to vote someone out or the game continues.

Guess who the Mole is before they figure out the word!
